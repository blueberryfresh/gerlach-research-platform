# Remote Participant Instructions

## Gerlach Personality Research Study - Participant Guide

**Welcome!** This guide will help you participate in our research study from anywhere using just your web browser.

---

- ✅ **A computer or laptop** (Windows, Mac, or Linux)
- ✅ **Internet connection** (stable connection recommended)
- ✅ **Web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- ✅ **Your Participant ID** (provided by the research team)
- ✅ **45-60 minutes** of uninterrupted time

### NOT Required:
- ❌ **NO GitHub account needed**
- ❌ **NO software installation needed**
- ❌ **NO technical knowledge needed**
- ❌ **NO downloads required**

## 📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Open Your Web Browser

1. Open your preferred web browser:
   - **Chrome** (recommended)
   - **Firefox**
   - **Safari** (Mac users)
   - **Edge** (Windows users)

2. Make sure your browser is up to date
   - Most browsers update automatically
   - If prompted, update before starting

---

### Step 2: Visit the Research Platform

1. **Type this URL** in your browser's address bar:
   ```
   https://gerlach-research.streamlit.app
   ```
   
   *(The exact URL will be provided by your research coordinator)*

2. **Bookmark the page** (optional but recommended)
   - Press `Ctrl+D` (Windows) or `Cmd+D` (Mac)
   - This saves the link for easy access

3. **Wait for the page to load**
   - You should see: "🔬 Gerlach Research Platform"
   - Loading may take 10-30 seconds on first visit

---

### Step 3: Registration

You'll see a welcome screen with instructions.

1. **Read the research information**
   - Purpose of the study
   - What you'll be doing
   - Time commitment (45-60 minutes)
   - Data privacy information

2. **Enter your Participant ID**
   - Type the ID provided by the research team
   - Example: `P001`, `P042`, `PARTICIPANT_123`
   - **Important**: Use the exact ID given to you

3. **Provide consent**
   - Check the box: "I consent to participate in this research study"
   - This confirms you understand and agree to participate

4. **Click "Begin Research Session"**
   - Your session will start
   - A unique session ID will be created

**Screenshot Example:**


### Step 4: Big Five Personality Assessment

You'll complete a 50-question personality assessment.

1. **Read the instructions carefully**
   - Rate each statement on a 1-5 scale
   - 1 = Strongly Disagree
   - 5 = Strongly Agree

2. **Answer honestly**
   - There are no "right" or "wrong" answers
   - Choose what best describes you
   - Take your time (10-15 minutes)

3. **Questions are organized by trait**
   - Click on each section to expand
   - Answer all questions in each section
   - Use the slider or radio buttons

4. **Submit your assessment**
   - Click "Submit Assessment" when done
   - You'll see your personality scores
   - Note your personality type

**Tips:**
- ✅ Answer based on how you generally are, not how you wish to be
- ✅ If unsure, choose "Neutral" (middle option)
- ✅ Don't overthink - go with your first instinct

---

### Step 5: Task Selection

Choose which task you'll work on with the AI.

1. **Select a Task**
   - **Noble Industries**: Evaluate and rank job candidates
   - **Popcorn Brain**: Creative brainstorming and problem-solving
   
   Choose the one that interests you more!

2. **Select an AI Personality**
   - **⚖️ Average**: Balanced and practical
   - **⭐ Role Model**: Optimistic and organized
   - **🎯 Self-Centred**: Direct and competitive
   - **🤫 Reserved**: Calm and conventional
   
   You can choose any personality - there's no wrong choice!

3. **Click "Start Task Dialogue"**
   - The task document will load
   - The chat interface will open
   - You're ready to collaborate!

---

### Step 6: Task Dialogue (Main Activity)

This is where you'll work with the AI to complete your chosen task.

#### How the Chat Works:

1. **Read the task document**
   - It's displayed on the screen
   - Take a few minutes to understand it
   - You can scroll through it anytime

2. **Chat with the AI**
   - Type your messages in the text box at the bottom
   - Press Enter or click Send
   - The AI will respond within a few seconds

3. **Work together**
   - Discuss the task
   - Share your ideas
   - Ask questions
   - Consider the AI's suggestions
   - Make decisions together

**What to Do:**
- ✅ Engage naturally, like talking to a colleague
- ✅ Share your genuine thoughts and opinions
- ✅ Ask questions if you're unsure
- ✅ Disagree with the AI if you think differently
- ✅ Take your time to think through your responses

**What NOT to Do:**
- ❌ Don't rush - quality over speed
- ❌ Don't just agree with everything the AI says
- ❌ Don't worry about grammar or typos
- ❌ Don't treat it like a test with "right answers"

**Example Conversation:**

```
⭐ AI: Hello! I'm excited to help you evaluate these 
candidates. Shall we start by reviewing the job requirements?

👤 You: Yes, that sounds good. The position requires 
strong leadership and technical skills.

⭐ AI: Great! Let's create a systematic approach. I suggest 
we evaluate each candidate on three criteria: leadership, 
technical skills, and cultural fit. Does that work for you?

👤 You: That makes sense. Let's start with Candidate A.

[Conversation continues for 20-40 messages...]
```

4. **When you're done**
   - Click "End Dialogue" button
   - This saves your conversation
   - You'll move to the next step

**Time**: Expect 15-30 minutes for this section

---

### Step 7: Task-Specific Response

Based on your task, you'll complete a short form.

#### If you chose Noble Industries:

1. **Rank the candidates**
   - Assign a rank (1, 2, 3, etc.) to each candidate
   - 1 = Your top choice
   - Each candidate must have a unique rank

2. **Provide rationale**
   - Explain WHY you ranked each candidate that way
   - Write 2-3 sentences per candidate
   - Be specific about your reasoning

3. **Submit rankings**
   - Review your rankings
   - Make sure all rationales are filled in
   - Click "Submit Rankings"

#### If you chose Popcorn Brain:

1. **Rate your creative performance**
   - Use sliders to rate yourself (1-7 scale)
   - Four dimensions:
     - **Originality**: Did you create unique ideas?
     - **Flexibility**: Did you present alternatives?
     - **Elaboration**: Did you add details to ideas?
     - **Fluency**: Did you create many ideas?

2. **Submit assessment**
   - Click "Submit Assessment"
   - You'll see automated metrics from your conversation
   - These show what the system detected in your dialogue

**Time**: 5-10 minutes

---

### Step 8: Post-Experiment Survey

You'll answer 37 questions about your experience.

#### Part 1: Rating Questions (1-31)

1. **Read each statement**
2. **Rate on 1-7 scale**
   - 1 = Strongly Disagree
   - 4 = Neutral
   - 7 = Strongly Agree
3. **Use the sliders** to select your rating

**Example Questions:**
- "I am confident that our work is done correctly"
- "I enjoyed the LLM-Human collaboration"
- "The LLM showed compassion towards me"

#### Part 2: Open-Ended Questions (32-37)

1. **Read each question carefully**
2. **Type your detailed responses**
3. **Be honest and specific**

**Example Questions:**
- "Describe any positive impacts from the LLM"
- "Discuss the compatibility of you and the LLM"
- "How did the LLM's personality play a role?"

**Tips:**
- ✅ Be honest - your feedback is valuable
- ✅ Provide specific examples when possible
- ✅ It's okay to be critical
- ✅ Write as much or as little as you want

4. **Submit Survey**
   - Click "Submit Survey" when complete
   - You can choose to do another task or finish

**Time**: 10-15 minutes

---

### Step 9: Complete Session

You're done! Here's what happens next:

1. **Generate Your Report**
   - Click "Complete Session & Generate Report"
   - Wait while the system creates your report (30-60 seconds)

2. **View Your Report**
   - See your personality profile
   - Review your dialogue statistics
   - Read your task-specific results
   - View survey summary

3. **Download Your Report** (optional)
   - Click "Download Markdown Report" or "Download HTML Report"
   - Save to your computer
   - You can keep this for your records

4. **Thank You!**
   - Your participation is complete
   - You can close the browser
   - The research team will contact you about compensation (if applicable)

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Problem: Page won't load

**Solutions:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different browser (Chrome recommended)
3. Clear your browser cache:
   - Chrome: `Ctrl+Shift+Delete` → Clear browsing data
   - Firefox: `Ctrl+Shift+Delete` → Clear recent history
4. Disable browser extensions temporarily
5. Try incognito/private mode

### Problem: Can't submit a form

**Solutions:**
1. Make sure all required fields are filled
2. Check for error messages in red
3. Scroll up to see if you missed any questions
4. Try clicking the submit button again
5. Refresh the page (your answers should be saved)

### Problem: AI is not responding

**Solutions:**
1. Wait 10-15 seconds (sometimes responses take time)
2. Check your internet connection
3. Refresh the page
4. If problem persists, contact support

### Problem: Lost my place / Browser closed

**Solutions:**
1. Return to the same URL
2. Enter your same Participant ID
3. The system should resume where you left off
4. If not, contact the research team

## 📱 Can I Use My Phone or Tablet?

**Not Recommended**

While the website *might* work on mobile devices, we **strongly recommend using a computer or laptop** because:

- ❌ Small screen makes reading task documents difficult
- ❌ Typing long responses is harder on mobile
- ❌ Some features may not display correctly
- ❌ Chat interface works best on larger screens

**If you must use mobile:**
- Use landscape orientation
- Use a tablet (larger than phone)
- Expect the study to take longer
- Be patient with typing
