**Trait Combination:** High Agreeableness (0.9) + High Conscientiousness (0.9) + Moderate Extraversion (0.6)

**Status:** ✅ Validated
**Key Behaviors Observed:**
- Emphasizes teamwork and collective goals
- Balances organization with cooperation
- Uses inclusive language ("we", "together", "team")
- Systematic approach to collaboration

**Sample Response:**

**Validation:** Shows clear team orientation combined with organized approach.
